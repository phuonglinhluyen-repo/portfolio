What I Learned

Writing for performance is writing for a live community.

A script has to land in real time. Every line had to carry weight on stage — that pressure taught me economy of language I bring to everything I write now.

Logistics is community management with physical stakes.

Coordinating 20 people backstage during a live show is the same skill as managing an online community — clear communication, pre-emptive planning, calm under pressure.
